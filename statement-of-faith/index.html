About Us

Statement of Faith

These core convictions shape who we are and how we live as a community of faith at Newark Christian Fellowship.

“For God so loved the world that he gave his one and only Son, that whoever believes in him shall not perish but have eternal life.”

John 3:16

01

The Scriptures

We believe in the Scripture of the Old and New Testament is given by the inspiration of God and is the supreme authority in all matters of faith and conduct.

02

The Triune God

We believe in one God eternally exists in three persons – the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit.

03

Jesus Christ

We believe that Jesus Christ our Lord is the God from eternity, the only begotten Son of God. We believe in the virgin birth, sinless life, atoning death and bodily resurrection, in His ascension to the Father, and in His return with power and glory.

04

The Holy Spirit

We believe that the Holy Spirit, the Third Person of the blessed Trinity, dwells in every believer and He is ever present to testify Christ.

05

Salvation

We believe our redemption has been accomplished solely by the blood of our Lord Jesus Christ, the salvation of the believer comes only through Jesus Christ, all who receive by grace through faith in the Lord Jesus Christ are born again of the Holy Spirit and become children of God.

06

Man and Sin

We believe that man was created in the image of God, that he fell through sin, and lost spiritual life which is separation from God.
